## Biography
Matt Dinniman is an American speculative fiction author and artist from Gig Harbor, Washington. Active in fantasy, science fiction, and LitRPG, he is best known for the bestselling Dungeon Crawler Carl series, which helped popularize LitRPG with its blend of brutal dungeon-crawl action, dark humor, and character-driven storytelling. Before writing full time, he worked as a traveling artist, creating pet portraits at animal and cat shows, an experience that later fed into his fascination with animals—especially the cat Princess Donut in Dungeon Crawler Carl.

Dinniman began writing early in life and developed his career over years of small-press and self-published work, including the Shivered Sky trilogy, the Dominion of Blades series, Kaiju: Battlefield Surgeon, and other apocalyptic or horror-tinged novels. Dungeon Crawler Carl started as web fiction on Royal Road and grew into a major commercial success, eventually picked up in print by Ace Books while Dinniman retained digital and audio rights. His success has positioned him as a leading figure in contemporary LitRPG, with his work expanding into audio, translation, and licensed formats.

Alongside his writing, Dinniman plays bass in rock and metal bands and continues to create visual art. Known online for a sardonic sense of humor and a strong relationship with his readership via platforms like Patreon, he has built a dedicated community around his fiction. His career illustrates the possibilities of independent publishing and cross-media storytelling in modern genre fiction.
