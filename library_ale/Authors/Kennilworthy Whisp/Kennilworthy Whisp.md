## Biography
Kennilworthy Whisp is a fictional British wizarding author known within the Harry Potter universe as the foremost authority on the magical sport of Quidditch. Presented as a meticulous researcher and enthusiast, he devotes his life to chronicling the game’s history, tactics, and legendary teams, helping to codify Quidditch lore for generations of witches and wizards. His writings are treated in-universe as standard references for anyone wishing to understand the sport’s evolution and cultural importance.

Whisp’s best-known work, “Quidditch Through the Ages” (also published in Spanish as “Quidditch a través de los tiempos”), is portrayed as a comprehensive guide that blends archival research, eyewitness accounts, and his own analytical commentary. Through this book he becomes a key figure in preserving the heritage of Quidditch and making its complex rules, regional variations, and famous matches accessible to a broad magical readership. Although specific details of his private life, relationships, and exact dates are not provided in the canon, he is consistently depicted as a passionate, somewhat scholarly figure whose legacy is tied to his contribution to the understanding of Quidditch.
